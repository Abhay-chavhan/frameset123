A black panther is the melanistic colour variant of the leopard and the jaguar. Black panthers of both species have excess black pigments, but their typical rosettes are also present.
Speed: Jaguar: 80 km/h, Leopard: 58 km/h, Tiger: 49 – 65 km/h
Gestation period: Jaguar: 93 – 105 days, Leopard: 90 – 105 days, Tiger: 93 – 112 days
Lifespan: Jaguar: 12 – 15 years, Leopard: 12 – 17 years, Tiger: 8 – 10 years
